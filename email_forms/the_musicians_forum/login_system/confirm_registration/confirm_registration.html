This has the potential to be useful for everyone from solo musicians to bands of all different styles looking for a place to further their art.

As a registered user you can...

Upload and store...

...your own recordings where you can make your material public or private, and search material other users have posted. Recordings can be grouped by project names to keep things organized or allow band mates to have access to the associated files.

Join or create chat rooms

Drag & drop recordings into the dialog. If several people drop pieces of music into the chat at the same time everyone with the play/que turned on will hear them in the same order. There are also plans to set up recording online; until then any music/video files you have can be uploaded.

Upcoming plans...

A little sound editing

Over-dubbing, adding effects and combining tracks are planned as well as way to jam online and then download tracks separately or in combination.

Live video jam sessions...

...will eventually be possible with a video feed for each of the band mates. Friends can sit always in on practice sessions, and from there it would not be hard to schedule a public performance now and then.

A little further into the future...

  • This website will have it's own internal messaging system. You can also send and receive texts and emails without sharing personal info, or have a text sent automatically when other band-mates login.
  • Creating a section for music lessons is one more possibility. Initially posted lessons will be free. This could be useful for teachers advertise what they offer for potential full time students. More details pending.
  • This will also be a place where open mics can be advertized, as well as paying venues like parties, weddings, bars, restaurants or even coffee places.
  • Once you are registered you will have the option to be notified as more of these phases are finished.
