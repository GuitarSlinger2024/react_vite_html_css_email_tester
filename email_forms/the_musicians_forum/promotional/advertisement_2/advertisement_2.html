THE MUSICIAN'S FORUM is open for business!..........................................................

A website dedicated to music and musicians

You are invited,

I said I would make a website for musicians. A few years later you can now register, log-in and maybe begin to share your music with more people than ever before. As the number of users begins to grow, yours will be the first recordings that they will be able to listen to!

As 'The Musicians Forum' continues to develop it has the potential to be useful for anyone looking for a place to further their art or to find others to jam with, and from solo musicians to bands of all different styles.

As a registered user you can...

Upload and store...

Upload your own recordings where you can make your material public or private, and search material already posted. Recordings can be grouped by project names to keep things organized and/or to allow band mates access to the associated files.

Join or create chat rooms

Drag & drop your recordings into a chat. If several people drop pieces of music into the chat at the same time everyone with the play/que turned on will hear them in the same order. There are also plans to set up recording online; until then any music/video files you have can be uploaded.

A little sound editing

Recording, adding effects, over-dubbing and combining tracks are all still being worked on, as well as jamming together online.

Live video jam sessions...

...will eventually hav a video feed for each of the band mates. Friends can always sit in on practice sessions, and from there it would not be hard to schedule a public performance now and then.

A little further into the future...

  • This website will have it's own internal messaging system. You can also send and receive texts and emails without sharing personal info, or have a text sent automatically when other band-mates login.
  • Creating a section for music lessons is one more possibility. Initially posted lessons will be free. This could be useful for teachers to advertise what they offer to find potential full time students. More details pending.
  • This will also be a place where open mics can be advertized, as well as paying venues like parties, weddings, bars, restaurants or even coffee places.
  • Once you are registered you will have the option to be notified as more of these phases are finished.

Hope to hear from you soon...
